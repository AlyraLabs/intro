[ Principles ]

Core Principles

We focus on making cross-chain infrastructure trustworthy, transparent, and built to last.

Security-first

Every transaction in Alyra is transparent and deterministic — it either settles exactly as intended or doesn’t execute at all. No blind signing, no unpredictable outcomes.

Simplicity & Modularity

One API/SDK replaces dozens of fragile integrations. Developers plug in only what they need, building on a composable and flexible execution stack.

Scalability & Transparency

Alyra grows with the ecosystem: adding new chains or liquidity providers is seamless, while every route and settlement remains visible and verifiable.

		tradeType: "EXACT_INPUT",

	});

	const quoteResponse = await fetch(
		"https://api.alyra.finance/v1/quotes" + params.toString(),

		}
	);

	const quote = await quoteResponse.json();
	console.log("Quote to swap 1000 USDC for WETH: ", quote);
