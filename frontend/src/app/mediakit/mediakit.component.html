Logo Versions

*primary

The main and preferred version of the Alyra logo.
Always use this variant when possible, as it
provides the highest readability and stays true
to Alyra’s core visual identity.

Primary usage is the white logo on black. Gradient
and black-on-white versions are allowed only as
alternatives when the primary cannot be used.

Suggested Usage

Use only the official Alyra logo.

Ensure proper spacing around the logo.

Use the emblem alone only when recognition is clear.

Improper
Logo Usage

Color

Do not alter the official logo colors. Use only approved palettes.

Proportions

Do not stretch, skew, or distort the logo.

Cropping

Do not crop the logo — it must always remain fully visible.

Blur

Do not apply blur or low-quality effects to the logo.

Shadow

Do not add shadows, glows, or extra effects.

Format Change

Do not change the official font in the Alyra wordmark.

Position

Do not rearrange or swap the positions of the logo text and symbol.

Gradient

Do not place the logo on gradient backgrounds.
